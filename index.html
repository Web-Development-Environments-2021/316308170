About Me
My name is Eldad Shilo. I was born in 15/10/1934 in Gedera.
During my militiary service in the IDF I served in "Mamram" unit.
After that, I studied structural engineering in the "Technion", Haifa.
Since then, I have been living in Haifa, and raised there my 3 children with my lovely wife, Miriam.
I try to keep up with technology, and one of my hobbies is to burn movies and music on CDs.
